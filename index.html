How to carve a jack-o-lantern

  1. Choose a large pumpkin and use a serrated knife to cut off the top.
  2. Scoop out the seeds and some of the flesh
  3. Using a marker draw a simple outline of your design on the pumpkin. Use a small serrated knife to cut out the design.
  4. Place a candle inside the pumpkin, light it, and place the top back on. You're all done!
